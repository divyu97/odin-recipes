Lemon Ginger Water

Description

Lemon water is a refreshing beverage made by infusing water with freshly squeezed lemon juice. Known for its bright, tangy flavor, it is often enjoyed for its potential health benefits, such as aiding digestion and providing a boost of vitamin C. This simple drink is also a popular choice for hydration and can be served cold or at room temperature, sometimes with added ice or herbs for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup lemon juice
  • 15 ½ cups filtered water, divided
  • 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled
  • 4 teaspoons honey
  • 4 quart-size mason jars (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Combine lemon juice, 1/2 cup water, ginger, and honey in a blender; blend for 20 to 30 seconds.
  3. Strain the mixture evenly into 4 quart-sized mason jars or a 1-gallon pitcher.
  4. Top off with remaining water and stir. Place lids on the jars or cover pitcher and store in the refrigerator.
